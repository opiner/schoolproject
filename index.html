Welcome to Virtuous College Calabar

Dear Parents/Guadians
Thank you very much for taking time to visit our website and on behalf of everyone at Virtuous College , We are delighted to welcome you whether you are thinking of joining us or are already a member of Virtuous Family. Virtuous College is a citadel of learning located in a serene suburb of Calabar Cross River State, Nigeria, offering an integrated curriculum based education to all children irrespective of their gender, race, ethnicity and religious inclinations.

By choosing Virtuous College for the education of your child/ward, you have entrusted our staff of professionals a responsibility to mould their character and advance their knowledge level in order to prepare them for higher academic adventures and successful careers later in life. Virtuous College have some of the most visionary, innovative, seasoned, highly professional and skillful educators you will find anywhere. They bring many years of cutting-edge teaching experiences to the classroom and laboratories as you will see during your ward’s time with us. The school is very excited to be a partner in your ward’s success, and will give him/her the right foundation to build upon.

Right from its inception, Virtuous College have been committed to providing an excellent learning environment for optimal teaching-learning encounters that is beyond the propaganda of having ultra-modern facilities. VIRCOL is, and has long been regarded, a top player in the education sector. The school has an impressive track record established over time. Our students achieve highly and many have gained admission into world-leading schools, colleges and universities.
As an institution operated by the Christian tenets, our commitment is not just to help our scholars achieve outstanding performances and impressive certification after completion of studies, but also their spiritual development in order for them to emerge well-rounded individuals holding their own places as agents of change in our day by day morally decadent society. And that has made us a pace-setter for morally sound education in the country.
We have an array of services to choose from and our dedicated administrative staff will be ready to assist you with anything. Class sizes are moderate, averaging 20-25 students, with highly personalized teaching methodologies tailored for individual and overall class good performances.

It is not all work at Virtuous College either. We have well-packaged extra-curricular activities to complement and enhance classroom experiences and provide students with opportunities for personal growth and enrichment. If you are looking for a school that offers sound education, international exposure, advanced teaching techniques, serene and conducive learning environment, affordable tuition fees, look no further. Virtuous College have what you are looking for.


Come join us and let’s become part of your child/ward’s success story.
